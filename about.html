Featured Sound: The Band Be Easy

Jacksonville-based band, Be Easy, lives up to its mellow mantra, weaving a unique blend of musical influences to create a sound that is as indescribable as it is infectious. Young men with old souls, this self-taught trio prides itself on its organic sound and delivers a live music experience that embodies their name.

Frontman and vocal phenom Aaron Grigsby brings a soulful passion to the microphone, reminiscent of the classic Motown hitmakers he was raised on, and his honeyed tone brings to mind Al Green and Gregg Allman. Instrumentally, the six string skills of oft-barefoot guitarist Ryan Emling deliver Easy’s groove-laden interpretations with a full, rich sound that belies his lone acoustic guitar. His nimble fingers exemplify the almost twenty years experience he brings to the band. Be Easy’s youngest member, Justin “JMac” Macklin anchors the group with innovative worldbeat explorations on the djembe and cajon, rounding out this diverse union of sounds.

Together since 2010, Be Easy began in a small college town in southern Illinois before relocating to Jacksonville’s music-friendly scene. Their grass roots “just love the music” approach has earned them several regular spots on the beach circuit, and last year, during the SXSW festival, an infamous impromptu performance in the middle of 6th Street drew thousands. Currently in the studio, Be Easy plans to release their first original album later this year.
