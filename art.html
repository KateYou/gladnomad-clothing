Featured Artist: gladnomad Artist Chris Sellers

How do you describe your style?

I feel that I don't really have much of a style. I just love to create anything when it comes to art. Whether it's the way I draw and paint or if its a certain concentration of a subject. I guess I just love to create art depending on my moods.

What is your approach to design?

I always approach design with inspirational ideas. Whether its an animal, plant, interest, hobby, place, etc.

What kind of materials do you use?

Mainly pencil and acrylic paint. But every once in awhile I use oil pastels and charcoal. I guess that kinda goes with my mood as well. Clay is pretty sick too because I love making sculptures.

Where you are from and when did you first discover your creative talents?

I was born in Melbourne, Florida and grew up in Jacksonville. I've always been into art from as long as I can remember. I've always loved creating stuff!

What inspires you to keep going and how do you keep yourself motivated?

Just my passion for art.

Describe yourself in five words.

That's hard... I'd say calm, happy, laid back, free spirited, and blessed.

Share with us something funny that has happened to you recently.

I had to give my cat a bath the other day cause she was so dirty... it took 4 of us to make it possible and it still didn't work out as expected. Ahaha

Any last words of advice to inspire up and coming artists?

Just express yourself through your art no matter what it is you like to do. Cause I still love doing what I do to this day. And don't let anybody try to stop you from doing it!
